Reflections on Dubinskii&#x27;s nonlinear compact embedding theorem;;; John W. Barrett,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Suli_E/0/1/0/all/0/1">Endre S&#xfc;li</a> ;;; We present an overview of a result by Ju. A. Dubinskii [Mat. Sb. 67 (109) (1965); translated in Amer. Math. Soc. Transl. (2) 67 (1968)], concerning the compact embedding of a seminormed set in $L^p(0,T; \mathcal{A}_0)$, where $\mathcal{A}_0$ is a Banach space and $p \in [1,\infty]$; we establish a variant of Dubinskii's theorem, where a seminormed nonnegative cone is used instead of a seminormed set; and we explore the connections of these results with a nonlinear compact embedding theorem due to E. Maitre [Int. J. Math. Math. Sci. 27 (2003)].
