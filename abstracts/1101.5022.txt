Eigenfunction estimates and embedding theorems for the Dunkl harmonic  oscillator;;; Jes&#xfa;s A. &#xc1;lvarez L&#xf3;pez,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Calaza_M/0/1/0/all/0/1">Manuel Calaza</a> ;;; Eigenfunction estimates and embedding results are proved for the Dunkl harmonic oscillator on the line. These kind of results are generalized to operators on $\R_+$ of the form $P=-\frac{d^2}{dx^2}+sx^2-2f_1\frac{d}{dx}+f_2$, where $s&gt;0$, and $f_1$ and $f_2$ are functions satisfying $f_2=\sigma(\sigma-1)x^{-2}-f_1^2-f_1'$ for some $\sigma&gt;-1/2$.
