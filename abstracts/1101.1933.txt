Layer lengths, torsion theories and the finitistic dimension;;; Fran&#xe7;ois Huard,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Lanzilotta_M/0/1/0/all/0/1">Marcelo Lanzilotta</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Mendoza_O/0/1/0/all/0/1">Octavio Mendoza</a> ;;; Let $\Lambda$ be an artinian ring. Generalizing the Loewy length, we propose the layer length associated with a torsion theory, which is a new measure for finitely generated $\Lambda$-modules.
