Stability of the Spin Glass Phase under Perturbations;;; Pierluigi Contucci,,  <a href="/find/math-ph,math/1/au:+Giardina_C/0/1/0/all/0/1">Cristian Giardina&#x27;</a>,  <a href="/find/math-ph,math/1/au:+Giberti_C/0/1/0/all/0/1">Claudio Giberti</a> ;;; We introduce and prove a novel linear response stability theory for spin glasses. The new stability under suitable perturbation of the equilibrium state implies the whole set of structural identities that characterize the spin glass phase.
